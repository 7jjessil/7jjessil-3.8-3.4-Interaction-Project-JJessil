About Us

We here at Safari Trip Advisory would like to ensure that you are completely prepared for your adventure on the wonderous plains of the African Safari and intend on informing you as much as we can on the various African animals and on what you should bring for your trip.

What to bring on your Trip

These are some of the most important things you may need on your safari trip

Legal Information

Legal Information

Ensure to bring your passport, itinerary any tickets, travel and insurance documents and keep it safe as it is vital to leave and go to the safari.

Medicine

Medicine

Put any prescription and preventive meds in hand luggage in case it gets lost in your main luggage and bring more than enough for each medicine type in case you want to spend more time on the safari.

Protection against Heat

Protection against Heat

If you wear prescription glass bring prescription sunglasses, along with a hat that goes all around not just the front and sunblock as you will be outside most of the time.

Clothes & Toiletries

Clothes & Toiletries

Do not forget to bring your toiletries and make sure that all your clothes are casual but conformable.

For the Memories

For the Memories

Make sure to bring a high-quality camera, that can record video and magnify large scale distance and ensure to bring binoculars to see animals far in the distance.

Technology & Music

Technology_&_Music

Bring as much music as you can to keep you entertained, when you can't see animals, bring country-specific adaptors and bring your laptop to download your images from the day.
